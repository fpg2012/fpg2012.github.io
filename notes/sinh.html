双曲函数和反双曲函数笔记

2022-07-28
〔note〕
#note #math， 双曲函数

定义

sinh⁡x=ex−e−x2 \sinh x = \frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{2} sinhx=2ex−e−x​

cosh⁡x=ex+e−x2 \cosh x = \frac {e^x + e^{-x}}{2} coshx=2ex+e−x​

考虑复变函数cos⁡x\cos xcosx和sin⁡x\sin xsinx沿着虚轴切开。由此可以理解一些性质

求导和积分

(sinh⁡x)′=cosh⁡x (\sinh x)' = \cosh x (sinhx)′=coshx

(cosh⁡x)′=sinh⁡x (\cosh x)' = \sinh x (coshx)′=sinhx

∫sinh⁡x=cosh⁡x+C \int \sinh x = \cosh x +C ∫sinhx=coshx+C

∫cosh⁡x=sinh⁡x+C \int \cosh x = \sinh x + C ∫coshx=sinhx+C

反函数

推导，设y=sinh⁡xy = \sinh xy=sinhx，那么

y=ex−e−x2⇔ 2yex=e2x−1⇔ e2x−2yex−1=0⇒ ex=2y+4y2+42=y+y2+1⇒ x=ln⁡(y2+1+y) \begin{aligned} & y = \frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{2} \\ \Leftrightarrow &\ 2ye^x = e^{2x} - 1 \\ \Leftrightarrow &\ e^{2x} - 2ye^x - 1 = 0 \\ \Rightarrow &\ e^x = \frac{2y + \sqrt{4y^2 + 4}}{2} = y + \sqrt{y^2 + 1} \\ \Rightarrow &\ x = \ln\left(\sqrt{y^2+1} + y\right) \end{aligned} ⇔⇔⇒⇒​y=2ex−e−x​ 2yex=e2x−1 e2x−2yex−1=0 ex=22y+4y2+4​​=y+y2+1​ x=ln(y2+1​+y)​

如果设y=cosh⁡xy = \cosh xy=coshx，那么

y=ex+e−x2⇔ 2yex=e2x+1⇔ e2x−2yex+1=0⇒ ex=2y+4y2−42=y+y2−1⇒ x=ln⁡(y2−1+y) \begin{aligned} & y = \frac{e^x+e^{-x}}{2} \\ \Leftrightarrow &\ 2ye^x = e^{2x} + 1 \\ \Leftrightarrow &\ e^{2x} - 2ye^x + 1 = 0 \\ \Rightarrow &\ e^x = \frac{2y + \sqrt{4y^2 - 4}}{2} = y + \sqrt{y^2 - 1} \\ \Rightarrow &\ x = \ln\left(\sqrt{y^2-1} + y\right) \end{aligned} ⇔⇔⇒⇒​y=2ex+e−x​ 2yex=e2x+1 e2x−2yex+1=0 ex=22y+4y2−4​​=y+y2−1​ x=ln(y2−1​+y)​

得到反函数的表达式

sinh⁡−1x=ln⁡(x2+1+x) \sinh^{-1} x = \ln\left(\sqrt{x^2 + 1} + x\right) sinh−1x=ln(x2+1​+x)

cosh⁡−1=ln⁡(x2−1+x) \cosh^{-1} = \ln\left(\sqrt{x^2 - 1} + x\right) cosh−1=ln(x2−1​+x)

反函数的求导

(sinh⁡−1x)′=1u2+1 (\sinh^{-1} x)' = \frac{1}{\sqrt{u^2 + 1}} (sinh−1x)′=u2+1​1​

(cosh⁡−1x)′=1u2−1 (\cosh^{-1} x)' = \frac{1}{\sqrt{u^2 - 1}} (cosh−1x)′=u2−1​1​
